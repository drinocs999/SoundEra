About us

We're a team of passionate students who created SoundEra to bring our favorite 80s and 90s bands to a whole new generation. Founded in 2025 at the National College of Ireland in Dublin, SoundEra is all about taking you on an epic journey through the greatest rock bands of all time. Ready to dive in and rock out?
